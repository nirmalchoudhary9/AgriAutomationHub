Livestock Monitoring System

Status: Idle

The Livestock Monitoring System is designed to keep track of the health and well-being of your livestock. It provides real-time data on their condition and allows you to take appropriate actions for their care.

Features of our system:

  • Monitoring of livestock health parameters such as body temperature, heart rate, and weight.
  • Alerts for abnormal conditions and potential illnesses.
  • Historical data analysis for trends and patterns in livestock health.
